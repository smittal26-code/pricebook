🚀 Install as App on Phone

🧠 Android
 iPhone / iPad
1
Open in Chrome
Make sure you are using Chrome browser (not Samsung Browser or other). Tap the link above to open the app.
2
Tap Install banner
Chrome shows a banner at the bottom: "Add Price Book to Home screen" — tap Install.
3
If no banner shows
Tap Chrome 3-dot menu (top right) → tap "Add to Home screen" → tap Add.
✓ App installs with gold gem icon on your home screen. Opens full screen like a real app — no browser bar.
1
Open in Safari
Tap the link above. Make sure it opens in Safari (not Chrome on iPhone — Chrome on iPhone cannot install PWA).
2
Tap the Share button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share icon (box with arrow pointing up ⇧).
3
Add to Home Screen
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen". Tap Add in top right.
✓ Price Book icon appears on home screen. Tap it — opens full screen with no Safari address bar.

☁ Connect Google Drive

1
Log into Gmail first
Open Chrome/Safari → go to gmail.com → sign in as sumitskjewellers@gmail.com. Do this before opening the app.
2
Open the app
Tap Price Book from home screen. Top right shows "Connecting..." then turns green "Drive synced".
3
Verify it works
Add one test entry → tap Save to Drive → open drive.google.com on your computer → look for file jewellery_pricebook_v4.json.
☁ All your data — suppliers, prices, images — saves automatically to sumitskjewellers@gmail.com Google Drive after every entry.

📌 Scanning Visiting Cards

1
Tap "Front side" box
Your phone camera or gallery opens. Take a clear photo of the front of the visiting card.
2
Tap "Back side" box
Same process for the back of the card (if it has address or more numbers).
3
Tap "Read with AI"
AI reads both sides together and fills in firm name, all phone numbers, address automatically. You just review and correct.
⚠ This does NOT work inside Claude.ai chat. Must be opened in Chrome or Safari on your phone directly.
